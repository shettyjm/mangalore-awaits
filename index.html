✦ Only in Mangalore

Signature Experiences

These are not just tourist activities — they are living traditions that define Mangalore's soul.

🥁
Yakshagana — The Living Epic
Witness the ancient all-night theatrical tradition of coastal Karnataka performed under the open sky. Elaborate costumes, percussion, song and story — Yakshagana is like nothing else on earth.
3–8 hrs Oct – May Cultural Village Venues
🎏
Kambala — Buffalo Races
The thrilling traditional buffalo race on slush tracks — Mangalore's answer to NASCAR, held in village paddy fields Nov–March.
🦞
Fish Market at Dawn
The 5AM Hoige Bazaar wholesale fish market is a riot of color, noise and fresh catch — one of India's most cinematic sights.
🛶
Backwater Village Cruise
A slow boat through coconut-lined backwaters of the Gurupura and Netravathi rivers as the sun dips low.
✦ Eat Like a Mangalorean

Food That Defines a City

Mangalorean cuisine is one of India's most celebrated — rich coconut-based curries, unmatched seafood and legendary street snacks.

Food Guide →
🦀
Mangalorean Fish Curry
Kori rotti, neer dosa or rice — the coconut-tamarind curry is the city's soul in a bowl.
🍗
Chicken Ghee Roast
A Kundapur original — fiery red masala, ghee and a slow roast that has conquered the world.
🥞
Neer Dosa
Paper-thin rice crepes, delicate as lace — the perfect companion to any Mangalorean side dish.
🥟
Goli Baje
Fluffy maida fritters with a hint of sourness — the quintessential Mangalore tea-time snack since forever.
🍵
Filter Coffee + Buns
The famous Udupi-style toddy shop buns with filter kaapi — a breakfast ritual that cannot be skipped.
